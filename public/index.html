What is Jilion

Jilion is a team of developers and designers based in Lausanne, Switzerland on the EPFL start-up campus. We provide you with our expertise in Internet software development and consulting services in addition to developing our own products.

We pride ourselves on delivering impactful and state-of-the-art solutions to your online business goals.

What we do

We have strong expertise in web application development and interface design. We can collaborate with your team in defining your product goals and specifications, its online presence and market strategy.

We are experts in the Ruby on Rails framework and the Facebook Platform and we can empower your product by adding advanced social media integration.

We can help you build cutting-edge applications for the iPhone and iPod touch that will enhance your brand and raise user satisfaction significantly.

We want to delight your users with easy-to-use applications and beautiful interfaces that will make the difference.

Our strength

We cover the full spectrum of competences, from solid back-end web services to sleek iPhone applications and web front-ends.
